About

My name is Ric Charles Paquibot, and I am a dedicated Website Developer with a strong passion for Front-End Development. I graduated with an Associate Degree in Diploma Computer Technology. During my On-the-Job Training (OJT), I gained valuable experience by freelancing in Web Development. I have hands-on experience in WordPress Development, as well as custom coding using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React. Following my graduation, I pursued further professional development by completing a Full-Time Full-Stack Web Development certificate program at KodeGO. This program spanned three months and provided me with real-world project experience. The training was intensive and practical, allowing me to refine my skills through hands-on learning. Additionally, I gained exposure to server-side programming using PHP and the Laravel framework, although I admit that I am not yet fully confident in this area. Having successfully completed the program, I am now eager to embark on an entry-level position as a web developer. I am motivated, determined, and ready to contribute my skills and knowledge to create exceptional digital experiences.
